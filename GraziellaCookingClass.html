Cooking Classes with Chef Graziella

A selected set of hands-on Tuscan cooking lessons. Each class lasts about 2 hours, and at the end guests taste the dishes they prepared.

Each participant receives a diploma and an English recipe booklet. Chianti Classico red wine, limoncello or grappa, and bread baked in a wood oven are included.
